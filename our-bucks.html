Ridley was born on the farm in March of 2018. After some debating whether to sell or retain him, we decided to retain him and breed him to our herd queen Jaala in the fall of 2018 and he did not disappoint. He gave us two doelings in March of 2019. He has grown into an impressive yearling buck and is already as tall as his sire Jagger and we are very excited to watch Ridley grow this year. He will be bred to Jaala and Felicite this fall breeding season!
