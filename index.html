Welcome to Lil Patch of Heaven!

Located in Southwest Tennessee, we are a small hobby farm that breed, raise, and care for our growing herd of Nubian goats. We are currently building our herd of Nubians and our goal is to improve our lines and become a complete purebred herd. Our herd tested negative for CAE in August 2019. We offer a variety of Nubians to fit your needs and/or budget. We offer Purebred Nubians, American Nubians, Graded Nubians, and Non-registered Nubians. All available babies being sold will come with CD&T, disbudding, and cocci prevention. Tattoos and registration papers will be provided with those able to be registered. Our breeding season runs from late August to late November. Our kidding season runs from late January to late April. The health and happiness of our goats is our number one priorty.
